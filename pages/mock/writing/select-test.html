📋 Writing Test Information

⏱️ Time Limit

You have 60 minutes total to complete both writing tasks. We recommend spending 20 minutes on Task 1 and 40 minutes on Task 2.

📝 Task 1

Write at least 150 words describing visual information such as charts, graphs, tables, or diagrams. Focus on key trends and comparisons.

📖 Task 2

Write at least 250 words in response to an argument, problem, or point of view. Present clear arguments with examples and evidence.

📧 Submission

Your completed essays will be automatically sent to your teacher for evaluation and feedback upon submission.
